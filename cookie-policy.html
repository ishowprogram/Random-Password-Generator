Cookie Policy

1. Use of Cookies

The Random Password Generator does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. Our service operates entirely client-side without the need for data storage or tracking.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to store information about your preferences, improve site functionality, and provide personalized content.

3. Why We Don't Use Cookies

Our password generator is designed to be completely private and secure. We achieve this by:

  • Operating entirely in your browser
  • Not tracking any user behavior
  • Not storing any user preferences
  • Not using any analytics tools

4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not include any third-party services that might place cookies on your device.

5. Questions

If you have any questions about our cookie policy, please feel free to contact us.

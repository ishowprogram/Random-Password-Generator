Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

The Random Password Generator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or generated passwords. All password generation occurs locally in your web browser.

2. Password Security

Generated passwords are:

  • Created entirely within your browser
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Never stored or logged anywhere
  • Immediately discarded when you leave the page

3. Browser Storage

We do not use cookies or any form of local storage to maintain user data or preferences.

4. Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to any data as we do not collect or store any information.

5. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
